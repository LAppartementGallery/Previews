Aria Lemaître (b. 1986, Lyon) lives and paints in Marseille, where her studio occupies a former tailor's workshop on the rue Saint-Ferréol. Her practice circles a single, narrow subject: rooms that someone has just left. Coats on chairs, half-drawn curtains, a folded newspaper, the weight of light on a wall that has held it for a hundred years.
