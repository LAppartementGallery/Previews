Aria

Lemaître

Aria Lemaître (b. 1986, Lyon) lives and paints in Marseille, where her studio occupies a former tailor's workshop on the rue Saint-Ferréol. Her practice circles a single, narrow subject: rooms that someone has just left.

The works are small in number — she completes between six and nine canvases a year — and dense in observation.
